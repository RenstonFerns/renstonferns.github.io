* block: markdown
  id: research
  content:
  title: "Research Focus"
  subtitle: "Circular carbon fuels, process modelling, LCA, TEA, and clean combustion"
  text: |
  My research focuses on developing scalable and regionally grounded pathways for low-carbon liquid fuels. I work at the intersection of **Aspen Plus process modelling**, **life cycle assessment**, **techno-economic analysis**, **CO₂ capture and utilization**, and **combustion assessment**.

  ```
  My doctoral work developed integrated biomass-to-e-methanol systems connecting fermentation, residue gasification, CO₂ capture, CO₂ hydrogenation, uncertainty analysis, and fuel performance evaluation. The broader goal is to assess fuel pathways not only by yield, but also by carbon utilization, life-cycle emissions, cost structure, and end-use performance.

  Going forward, I aim to extend this work toward dynamic LCA/TEA, AI-assisted process assessment, digital-twin-enabled sustainability platforms, and decision-support tools for CO₂-to-X and biomass-derived fuel systems.
  ```
